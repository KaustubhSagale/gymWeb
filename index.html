Pricing

CrossFit

Crossfit is a form of intense physical activity that combines elements of strength training, cardio and gymnastics. At the core of CrossFit is the concept of functional training aimed at developing universal physical abilities such as strength, endurance, speed and flexibility.

TRX

Total Resistance exercise, which means training with the use of special TRX slings. It is a form of exercise that uses your own body weight and gravity to develop strength, endurance, flexibility and coordination. STRX training usually includes stretching, pull-ups, squats and other movements that activate different muscle groups. It can be used as an independent form of training or included in a fitness program.

Functional training

Functional training is focused on improving the physical abilities necessary for everyday activities. They include movements that collectively use muscles, joints, and core stability to improve strength, stability, flexibility, and coordination.

Stretching

Stretching is the practice of stretching muscles and tissues to improve flexibility, relax the body, and support general health. This includes controlled movements and postures aimed at stretching the muscles and increasing the range of motion in the joints.
